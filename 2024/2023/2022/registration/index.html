Registration Announcement

Hello GGSO Community,

Thank you all for your patience and flexibility regarding all the recent changes. The new confirmed date for this year’s tournament is Saturday, February 12, 2022, so mark your calendars! Like before, it is still scheduled to take place in-person on UC Berkeley campus.

Registration will open on October 18, 2021 at noon PDT on Scilympiad. There will be a limit of 50 teams with up to two teams from each school, and it will be done on a first come, first serve basis.

The registration fee for this year’s tournament will be $200 due by November 19th, 2021 and can be paid through PayPal or via check. If registration costs prevent your school from competing, please email us. Detailed information on how to register can be found here.

If you have any questions, feel free to email us at goldengatescioly@gmail.com!
